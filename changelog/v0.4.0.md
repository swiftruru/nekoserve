# NekoServe v0.4.0 — Autonomous Cat Agents

Released: 2026-04-13

Cats are no longer a shared pool that customers queue for. Instead every cat is now its own long-running agent that wanders between seated customers on its own timing — matching how a real cat café actually works. Customers no longer have a "wait for cat" phase; they dine normally and whatever cat visits happen during their stay is what they get.

## 🐈 Autonomous cat model

- **Each cat is an independent SimPy process** that loops: idle for `catIdleInterval` minutes (exponential), pick a random currently-seated customer, walk to their seat, stay for `catInteractionTime` minutes (normal), leave; then with probability `catRestProbability` enter a rest period before going back to idle. Cats have stable identities throughout the run.
- **Customers are visited, not visiting**. The customer lifecycle is now: arrive → wait for seat → seated → order → prepare → dine → wait for any cats currently on the customer to finish their visit → leave. A customer may be visited by zero, one, or several cats simultaneously during their stay.
- **"Cats keep people seated"**: a customer cannot stand up while any cat is still on their lap, even after dining is over. This matches reality (and makes seats a more interesting bottleneck when cats are friendly) but means `avgTotalStayTime` is influenced by cat behaviour, not just service times.
- **Cat rest** now belongs to an individual cat identity, not to "the last customer who triggered it". Rest events carry the cat's `resourceId` so the renderer can flip the right cat sprite to 💤.

## 🔁 Event schema change (breaking)

Three events are **gone**: `CUSTOMER_WAIT_CAT`, `CUSTOMER_START_CAT_INTERACTION`, `CUSTOMER_FINISH_CAT_INTERACTION`.

Two events are **new**:

| Event | `customerId` | `resourceId` | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `CAT_VISIT_SEAT` | customer being visited | `貓-N` (cat id) | Cat just arrived at the customer's seat |
| `CAT_LEAVE_SEAT` | customer being visited | `貓-N` (cat id) | Cat is wandering off |

`CAT_START_REST` / `CAT_END_REST` still exist, but their `resourceId` now carries the cat's identity rather than a customer id. Event count: 15 → 14.

## 📊 New metrics

`SimulationResult.metrics` gains two fields:

- **`avgCatVisitsPerCustomer`** — average number of cat visits received per served customer. A natural measure of "cat friendliness" at the current config.
- **`noCatVisitRate`** — share of served customers who left without a single cat encounter. Climbs when cats are lazy (`catIdleInterval` high) or when all cats are busy with other customers.

`catInteractionRate` is kept but its semantics sharpen to **"share of served customers who got at least one cat visit"** (equivalent to `1 - noCatVisitRate`).

## ⚙️ New config parameter

- **`catIdleInterval`** (default **4.0** minutes, Exponential mean) — controls how often each cat decides to initiate a new visit. Lower values make cats clingier; higher values make them lazy. All three preset scenarios ship with tuned defaults: Weekday 4.0, Holiday Rush 3.0, Cat Nap 6.0.

## 🎞️ Playback animation updates

- The `Cat queue` zone is **gone** from the scene layout — it no longer exists in the model.
- Each cat now has its own sprite that lives in the cat zone and **physically animates out to a customer's seat** when a visit starts, sitting next to the customer with a 💖 overlay, then walks back when the visit ends. Cats wake into 💤 when resting.
- Customer avatars swap to 😺 whenever any cat is currently on their lap, regardless of whether they're mid-dining or mid-wait.
- New speech bubble kind `catArrive` (`貓來了 💕` / `Cat here 💕`) replaces the old `petting` bubble.
- New scene counter: **cumulative cat visits so far** is shown under the kitchen zone.

## 🔧 Internals

- `shared/contracts/types.ts` updated: `EventType` union narrowed+widened, `SimulationConfig` gains `catIdleInterval`, `MetricSummary` gains `avgCatVisitsPerCustomer` and `noCatVisitRate`.
- `src/renderer/src/utils/replay.ts` rewritten: `CatSlot` is now indexed by 0-based cat id (parsed from `貓-N`) rather than customer id; `CustomerRuntime` grows a `visitingCats: Set<number>` field; `CustomerStage` no longer has `waitingCat` or `interacting`.
- `simulator-python/simulator/core.py` rewritten: cats are processes, customers maintain a shared `seated[]` dict so cats can find targets, and customer phase 5 polls visitors every 0.1 sim-minute before releasing the seat.
- `test_golden.py` updated: removed the 3 customer-cat events from `VALID_EVENT_TYPES`, added two new tests (`test_cat_visits_happen_autonomously`, `test_cat_visit_events_carry_cat_id_in_resource`), added the two new metric fields to the required set.

## ⬆️ Upgrade notes

- Version bumped `0.3.0` → `0.4.0`. This is a **breaking change**: simulation result JSON from v0.3 cannot be replayed by the v0.4 reducer because the three old cat events are unknown. Re-run your simulations after upgrading.
- Any external tooling that parses the event log must drop the three removed event types and add the two new ones.
- Custom scenarios saved in `localStorage` from v0.3 will still load, but `catIdleInterval` will come through as `undefined` — the UI default-fills it to 4.0 on first edit. Re-save to bake it in.
